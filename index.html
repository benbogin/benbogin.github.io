Ben Bogin

Post-doc

AI2/UW

Hi! I’m Ben, a post-doc at AI2 and the University of Washington. I have pursued my PhD at Tel Aviv University, under the guidance of Prof. Jonathan Berant, where I studied compositional generalization in semantic parsing and visual question answering.

I’m always open to new collaborations, feel free to reach out!
